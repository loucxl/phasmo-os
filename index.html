🧠 SANITY SYSTEM

Starting Sanity

  • Amateur - Nightmare: 100%
  • Insanity: 0%

Passive Drain

  • Lights OFF: -0.12%/s
  • Lights ON: 0%/s - Sanity can still be drained during events and hunts*
  • Near Firelight/Candle: Reduced (amount depends on tier)

Hunt Thresholds

  • Yokai: 80% (if talking nearby)
  • Thaye: 75% → 15% (ages over time)
  • Demon: 70% (can hunt any sanity rarely)
  • Raiju: 65% (near active electronics)
  • Onryo: 60% (any after 3 flames blown)
  • Mare: 60% (dark) / 40% (light)
  • Standard: 50%
  • Deogen: 40%
  • Shade: 35%
  • Banshee: Target's sanity only

Restoration

  • Sanity Pills: +25%
  • Tarot Sun: +100%

Ghost Speeds

  • Player Sprint: 3.0 m/s
  • Revenant (LoS): 3.0 m/s
  • Deogen (close): 0.4 m/s
  • Jinn/Raiju (boosted): 2.5 m/s
  • Hantu (cold): up to 2.7 m/s

Hunt Duration

🔮 CURSED POSSESSIONS

Ouija Board

Ask questions. 5-25% sanity per question. Say "Goodbye" or hunt!

Music Box

Wind to summon. Ghost walks toward you. DON'T hold until empty!

Summoning Circle

Light all 5 candles = ghost appears, then ~5 second grace before cursed hunt. Warning: lighting final candle at <16% sanity skips event and starts an immediate hunt.

Voodoo Doll

10 pins. Each = interaction. HEART PIN = HUNT!

Monkey Paw

5 uses (fingers). 9 possible wish types, each usable once per contract. Every wish has a curse/penalty. Very risky.

Tarot Cards

Random effects. Tower/Death = hunt. Sun = full sanity.

Haunted Mirror

Shows the ghost's favourite room (not current location — that's the Ouija Board). MASSIVE sanity drain (7.5%/s). Breaks and triggers cursed hunt if sanity runs out while using it.

📋 STRATEGY GUIDE

🎯 EARLY GAME (Setup Phase)

  • Locate ghost room using thermometer sweeps (coldest room)
  • Place equipment strategically: EMF reader, writing book, DOTS
  • Work as team to cover more ground faster
  • Manage sanity - stay in lit areas during setup
  • Use motion/sound sensors on large maps to track roaming
  • Identify 1-2 evidence pieces before pushing deeper

⚡ MID GAME (Evidence Collection)

  • Saturate ghost room with all relevant detection tools
  • Use smudge sticks to prevent hunts while investigating
  • Complete easy objectives first (photos, motion sensor)
  • Monitor sanity closely - know your hunt threshold
  • Communicate evidence found and suspected ghosts with team
  • Test specific ghost abilities (smudge timer, salt footprints)

🏁 LATE GAME (Identification & Exit)

  • With 3 evidence, identify ghost and GET OUT
  • Don't get greedy with objectives at low sanity
  • Use crucifixes and smudge sticks defensively
  • Have escape routes planned from all locations
  • Solo players: Know your hiding spots and exit paths
  • If unsure between 2 ghosts, use behavior tests

🎓 ADVANCED TECHNIQUES

  • Master ghost-specific tests (Spirit 180s, Wraith no prints, etc)
  • Use cursed possessions strategically for bonus evidence/photos
  • Learn looping patterns for fast ghosts (Revenant, Jinn)
  • Understand LoS mechanics - break vision to reset chase
  • On Nightmare, use behavior + 2 evidence to eliminate options
  • Practice solo investigations to understand ghost behavior better
  • Use parabolic mic to identify Banshee (unique wail) — but hearing Moroi sounds curses you (doubles sanity drain), so be ready to take pills
  • Leverage ghost weaknesses (Mare light sensitivity, etc)

⚠️ SURVIVAL TIPS

During Hunts

  • Break line of sight immediately
  • Hide in closets/lockers (close door, stay quiet) — except vs Deogen, which always finds you regardless
  • Loop furniture vs slow ghosts
  • Smudge to blind and slow ghost
  • Turn OFF electronics (flashlight, etc)
  • Don't talk - ghost hears you

Sanity Management

  • Stay in lit areas when possible
  • Light candles for safe zones
  • Save pills for after evidence
  • Avoid staring at Phantom (extra sanity drain) — take a photo during events to make it vanish
  • Leave before critical (25%) sanity
  • Use pills to slow Moroi

Equipment Usage

  • Place crucifixes in ghost room center
  • Smudge in ghost room for 90s safety
  • Drop EMF reader rather than carry it — carrying active equipment attracts Raiju and contributes to its speed boost
  • Use glowsticks for constant UV coverage
  • Salt in doorways tracks roaming
  • Multiple cameras = better Orb detection

Map-Specific

  • Small maps: Quick in-and-out
  • Medium maps: Use sensors to track
  • Large maps: Split team, motion sensors essential
  • Prison: Bring EXTRA lights
  • Maple/Campsite: No true safety
  • Asylum: Can take 20+ minutes

Insanity: 1 evidence shown, 2 hidden
Forced evidence on ghost cards = always shown regardless of difficulty

🎭 IN-GAME SCENARIOS

Something strange is happening and you don't know what it means? Find your situation below.

🚨 Ghost is hunting way too early▼

Suspects by threshold: Yokai 80% (if talking near ghost), Demon 70% (consistent), Thaye young 75% (only early game), Onryo 60% baseline or any sanity after 3 flame blow-outs, Mare 60% in darkness, Raiju 65% near electronics.

  • Rule out Yokai first: Stop all talking. If early hunts continue in complete silence, it's not Yokai — unless you're within 2m of it.
  • Distinguish Demon from Thaye: Thaye starts aggressive then progressively calms over 10+ minutes, while in the ghost room. Demon stays consistently aggressive throughout.
  • Confirm Demon: Smudge it, start timer. If it hunts 60–90s later (not 90s+), it's Demon. Also: Demon crucifix range is 50% larger: T1 4.5m, T2 6m, T3 7.5m — if one burns from across a room, very suspicious.
  • Onryo vs Demon: Put Firelights near the ghost. If hunts stop while flames are lit and resume only when blown out, it's Onryo not Demon.
  • Survival: Demon has only 20s between hunts. Place crucifixes early. Smudge only gives 60s of protection — less than usual.
🏃 Ghost speed feels wrong▼
  • Fast with breaker ON, normal with breaker OFF: Jinn. Turn the power off mid-hunt to confirm the speed drop.
  • Fast near your equipment, slower elsewhere: Raiju. Turn off flashlights and pick up gear — speed should normalise.
  • Extremely slow while searching, then sprints when it sees you: Revenant (1 m/s → 3 m/s). Break LoS around a corner immediately.
  • Fast in cold rooms, slow in warm: Hantu. Keep breaker ON to heat the map. Look for frosty breath clouds from the ghost during hunts.
  • Gets faster across multiple hunts as sanity drops: Moroi. Pills remove the curse and will slow it down temporarily — confirm this.
  • Very fast at start, clearly slower after 10+ minutes: Thaye. It's aging. Players need to be nearby for it to age.
  • Fast from distance, nearly stops right next to you: Deogen (3 m/s → 0.4 m/s). Never hide — loop it around furniture.
  • Speed alternating noticeably between hunts: Twins — one twin is slower (~1.5 m/s) and one faster (~1.9 m/s), alternating who hunts.
  • Speeds up when you move, slows when you freeze: Dayan. Stand completely still during a hunt to confirm the dramatic slow-down.
🔇 Ghost is barely doing anything▼
  • Almost no activity at all, especially at high sanity: Classic Shade. Activity increases as sanity drops, but it still won't hunt while any player is in the same room.
  • Confirm Shade: Drop everyone's sanity below 35% and group up in the ghost room with a crucifix. If it refuses to hunt while grouped, that's a strong Shade indicator. Go solo to trigger events.
  • Was very busy early, now almost silent: Thaye that has aged fully. Consistent calm after an aggressive opening = Thaye, not Shade.
  • Quiet phases interrupted by sudden activity bursts: Obambo. Wait and watch — if it cycles back to aggressive every ~2 minutes, that's not a Shade.
  • Solo tip: Use the Parabolic Mic — Myling produces more paranormal sounds than average between hunts even when otherwise quiet.
🕯️ Lights or flames are behaving strangely▼
  • Lights constantly switching off, never on: Mare. It cannot turn lights ON — only off. Keep the ghost room lit to drop its hunt threshold from 60% to 40%. Note: the Mare does not actively seek dark rooms — it simply roams more when its current room is lit, so you'll often see it moving around a lot.
  • Confirm Mare: Turn a light on and watch it immediately go off. This pattern alone is very strong. Note: Mare can still interact with radios and TVs (though it currently cannot turn them on — a known bug) — it's specifically room light switches it turns off.
  • Firelights being blown out regularly: Onryo. Each blow-out is a blocked hunt attempt. Keep 2 Firelights active — when one blows out, the second blocks the follow-up hunt.
  • Confirm Onryo: Place a crucifix AND a Firelight both within range. If the flame blows out instead of the crucifix burning, it's Onryo — flames take priority over crucifixes for Onryo hunts.
  • Lights going both on AND off randomly: Not Mare (Mare only turns off). Could be general ghost interactions or a Jinn near the breaker. Check power state.
🎙️ Sound or voice-related weirdness▼
  • One player gets chased every hunt while others are ignored: Banshee. It picks a single target at contract start. Keep the target's sanity above 50% — the Banshee checks only their sanity. Tip: crucifixes have 5m range vs Banshee (same as Demon), so they cover more ground.
  • Unique wail on Parabolic Mic (not a standard whisper): Banshee confirmed — no other ghost makes this sound.
  • Hunts starting while team is talking: Suspect Yokai. Stop all voice communication near the ghost room. If hunts stop, it was Yokai. If they continue, rule it out.
  • Ghost ignores your voice/flashlight from more than 2–3m during a hunt: Yokai — detection range drops to 2.5m during hunts. Talk loudly from 4m+ to test.
  • Sanity draining even in lit rooms: Moroi curse — someone triggered it via Spirit Box or Parabolic Mic. Take Sanity Pills immediately — pills are the only cure.
  • Ghost footsteps and vocals silent until it's almost on top of you: Myling — footsteps AND vocals only audible within 12m vs 20m for others. The Myling can still make sounds during hunts, they're just short-range. Drop a flashlight: if it flickers but you can't hear anything yet, that's Myling.
🧂 Physical evidence is telling you something▼
  • Salt pile disturbed, NO UV footprints: Wraith confirmed. Only ghost that never leaves UV prints in salt. Also: EMF 2 reading appearing near a player far from the ghost room = Wraith teleport.
  • 6-finger handprint on a door: Obake confirmed. No other ghost can do this. UV is Obake's forced evidence — will always appear on Nightmare.
  • UV fingerprints appearing less often or fading fast (around 30s): Suspect Obake — only 75% chance to leave prints, and halves the fade timer. Watch for model-change flickering during hunts too.
  • Outside/exit door closes fully without a hunt or event: Yurei confirmed. Only the Yurei can close exit doors outside of hunts. Watch for a 15% sanity drop alongside it.
  • Interior door closes smoothly (not during a hunt) and sanity drops: Likely Yurei ability. Smudge the room and put a motion sensor at the doorway — if it doesn't leave for ~90s, confirms Yurei weakness.
  • Salt disturbed WITH UV footprints: Rules out Wraith entirely. You're now looking at any ghost with UV in its evidence list.
🧠 Still stuck between 2–3 suspects▼

Use these hard eliminators — if you can observe any of these, cross a ghost off immediately.

  • Airball mist event happened — visually? → Eliminate Oni. You must actually see the floating mist ball. Walking manifestation events make the same hiss sound — the hiss alone is not enough to rule out Oni.
  • D.O.T.S silhouette visible to naked eye? → Eliminate Goryo — its silhouette is camera-only. (Note: on 0-evidence custom, Goryo shows no D.O.T.S at all.)
  • Ghost stepped in salt and LEFT UV prints? → Eliminate Wraith — Wraith never leaves UV prints in salt.
  • Ghost hunted while you were grouped in the ghost room? → Eliminate Shade — it cannot initiate a hunt when any player shares its room.
  • Ghost found you in a closet or locker immediately? → Strong Deogen — no other ghost reliably seeks out hidden players.
  • Smudge block lasted exactly 90s? → Eliminates Spirit (180s) and Demon (60s).
  • Smudge block lasted at least 180s consistently? → Strong Spirit indicator — Spirit always waits this long. Note: other ghosts can occasionally wait 180s by chance, so use the double-smudge method to confirm.
  • Ghost hasn't changed speed or activity after 10+ minutes with players nearby? → Eliminate Thaye (would have slowed) and Obambo (would have phase-switched).
  • 3 flames blown out but no hunt followed? → Eliminate Onryo — it always triggers a hunt on the 3rd blow-out (unless blocked by another flame or crucifix).
  • Got 3 evidence pieces + Ghost Orbs on Nightmare? → Mimic confirmed — no other ghost produces 4 evidence.

⚠️ DANGER TIERS ON NIGHTMARE/INSANITY

Knowing what you're up against changes how aggressively you should investigate.

🔴 Extreme — Investigate fast, exit often

  • Demon — 70% threshold, 20s cooldown, 60s smudge window
  • Deogen — knows your location always, hiding is useless
  • Thaye (young) — 75% threshold, 2.75 m/s early on
  • Onryo — any-sanity hunt after 3 flame blow-outs
  • Gallu — provoked and made more dangerous by your own protective gear

🟠 High — Respect it, manage sanity

  • Revenant — 3 m/s on LoS, nearly uncatchable once seen
  • Moroi — fastest ghost in game at 0% sanity (3.71 m/s)
  • Raiju — 2.5 m/s near electronics, hunts at 65%
  • Yokai — 80% threshold if your team is talking
  • Obambo — unpredictable; safe then suddenly lethal

🟡 Medium — Stay aware

  • Jinn — 2.5 m/s with breaker on, turn it off
  • Mare — 60% in darkness, keep the lights on
  • Hantu — 2.7 m/s in freezing rooms
  • Myling — silent approach, near-zero warning time
  • Dayan — punishes movement, freeze to survive

🟢 Lower — Still kill you, just more predictable

  • Shade — 35% threshold, won't hunt in groups
  • Spirit — no special hunt mechanics, easy to smudge-stall
  • Banshee — safe for non-targets in multiplayer
  • Thaye (aged) — becomes 15% threshold and 1 m/s
  • Deogen — 0.4 m/s up close, consistently loopable
